Play Local: exploring games made in Seattle.

Play Local is a series of zines containing interviews with local indie game developers, how-to guides for getting started as an indie game developer, and guides to all the indie video game things in the Seattle area.

Later these zines will be compiled into a book! We'll also offer the zines & book bundled with some of the games made by local indie developers!

Do you live in Seattle and make video games? We should interview you! Tell us more about you and your games!

Be the first to find out about new releases:


We only send email when there's super important updates, and we won't sell your info.

Games on SeattleWiki

Looking for a guide to all things "games in Seattle"?

Go look at SeattleWiki.net/Games.

As we research the games being made in Seattle, we use SeattleWiki as a kind of "open reporter's notebook".

You can follow along with new info, and even add info about your own games or games made in Seattle that you know about by visiting seattlewiki.net/games and editing that page!
